Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear?

+

We've requested formal attire for our celebration. For ladies, the ceremony will be held on grass, so we recommend wedges or block heels. Gentlemen, suits or tuxedos are perfect.

Where should I stay?

+

We've reserved room blocks at The Charleston Harbor Hotel and The Historic District Inn. Booking information and special rates are available on our accommodations page. Both hotels are approximately 15 minutes from the venue.

Is there transportation provided?

+

Yes! Shuttle service will be provided from the recommended hotels to the venue and back. The shuttles will depart at 3:15 PM and return at 12:30 AM. Valet parking is also available at the venue.

Can I bring my children?

+

While we adore your little ones, we've chosen to make this an adults-only celebration. We hope this gives all our guests a chance to relax and enjoy the evening. Thank you for understanding.

What if I have dietary restrictions?

+

Please let us know about any dietary restrictions or food allergies when you RSVP. Our catering team is experienced in accommodating various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other requirements.

Will the ceremony be indoors or outdoors?

+

The ceremony will be held outdoors in the garden pavilion. We have a beautiful indoor backup plan in case of inclement weather. The reception will take place in the estate's grand hall.
