About Me

My name is Diana. I am originally Hungarian–Ukrainian, married to a wonderful German-French Attorney At Law, Owner and Managing Partner of the international law firm Gobert Tax & Legal, Chairman of the German Business Association in Hungary, and we are proudly based in the breathtaking, cinematic city of Budapest, Hungary.

Rooted in a deep belief in love, family values, art, and refined aesthetics, our lives and careers have been shaped by extensive international experience. Having traveled the world and resided in several countries including Germany, South of France and Italy, we understand how to celebrate life's most meaningful moments with elegance, authenticity, and intention.

My Approach

Our clients come from abroad, representing diverse cultures, nationalities, and backgrounds. For this reason, we place exceptional importance on personalized customer service, transparency, and professional integrity. Every couple deserves to feel fully understood, comfortable, and confident that their unique vision will be brought to life with care and precision.

That's why I see myself not only as a wedding planner, but as an entrepreneur and creative partner—an approach that is reflected in every wedding and event we curate.
